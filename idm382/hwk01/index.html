Cool Mist Humidifiers






Price and Availability


It costs $65 and is currently on sale for $33.99 and at least 7 days of free delivery on the Amazon website.

What does this technology do?


This device, known as a humidifier, is mostly used in bedrooms. It functions by blowing a tiny spray of water vapor into a space to raise its humidity level. This helps in the fight against dry air, which is useful in bedrooms to enhance sleep and reduce respiratory irritation. Furthermore, it appears that this product is a top-fill diffuser, making refilling and using it simpler.

What do you want to do with technology?

I plan to improve this technology to include adaptive scheduling that remembers the users' feelings and habits, automatically changing humidity levels and other app settings to maximize comfort. According to the app, a few do not exist, and I realized I might make this app update with extra features of historical data monitoring, user profile, Thermometer, Hygrometer, and suggestions for optimal humidity levels for different situations. Another idea was to include a safety function, such as an automatic shutdown when tripped over by a child or a pet. Furthermore, a quality feedback system could monitor the air quality and communicate with the smartphone in the room. If the air quality falls below a certain threshold, the connected devices will work together to enhance the air quality.

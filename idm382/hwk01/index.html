Additionally, users have the option to speak or input words into the app to transform them into morse code. In reality, a lot of students post encouraging words like I love food and Smile on the building window to make their dorm room windows enjoyable. My second idea is to enhance the prototype by adding a motion detector that would allow Strip lED to measure mood based on interactions with smart devices or even facial expressions captured by embedded cameras. It's possible that the voice or gesture might identify the user's mood and instruction. In addition, adding the ability to view historical data monitoring for energy efficiency could help users understand their energy consumption.
